About Udyamee

We are an innovative platform that connects rural entrepreneurs with urban entrepreneurs, helping them meet everyone's needs for successful production. We also offer a platform that provides business education, mentorship and training to rural entrepreneurs. Join us today to help build the future of entrepreneurship in India

Our Motive

Making a positive change in our world motivates us. We know you feel the same and we want to inspire and empower you to make a difference. We want to help you create a peer network of inspiration and support.
